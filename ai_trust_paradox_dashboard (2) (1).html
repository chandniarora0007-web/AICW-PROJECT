High trust rate by experience band
Trust FALLS as experience RISES — the expertise effect
Early Career: 10% trust. Mid-Early: 5.4%. Mid: 2.8%. Experienced: 2.3%.
Experienced (10+ yrs) = lowest trust 2.3% AND highest distrust 49.8%. Deeper expertise → greater ability to detect AI errors → lower trust.

Distrust by tool count

1 Tool: 41.4% distrust. 2-3 Tools: 41.4%. 4+ Tools: 47.5%.

Threat rate by experience band — opposite of Paradox 1
In P1: trust falls with experience. In P3: threat ALSO falls with experience. Early career most threatened, experienced least.
Threatened rate: Early Career 21.2%, Mid-Early 17.7%, Mid 14.6%, Experienced 13.6%.
Cross-paradox insight: Experienced devs DISTRUST AI most (P1: 49.8% distrust) but feel LEAST threatened (P3: 13.6%). Deep expertise → scepticism of output AND security in own irreplaceability.
